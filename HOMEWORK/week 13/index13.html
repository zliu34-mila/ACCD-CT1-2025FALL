build a multi-channel music controller

I used musical monophony and audio to create an interactive music console. I used a slide, buttons, a potentiometer, and a joystick to achieve the final music control. The buttons control four separate channels of monophonic sounds, while the potentiometer controls three complete background tracks that can be mixed into the final music. My slide plays the intro music when its value is 0 and the potentiometer value is 0. As the value changes, it corresponds to different music channels. My joystick controls two separate mixed sound elements. In terms of visual presentation, I drew an image of a prism that can change in texture and color according to the music.

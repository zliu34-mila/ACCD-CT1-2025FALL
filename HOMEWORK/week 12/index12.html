build a milti-channel controller

In this assignment, I used a thumbstick as the component to control the screen. At first, when setting up the stick, I couldn't figure out its direction and pin connections, so I wrote a piece of test code to help me determine the wiring of the pins. Then, I controlled the movement direction of the ball on the screen by moving the thumbstick up, down, left, and right, and I set boundaries for the ball so that it wouldn't go off the screen. My button is used to switch the color of the ball.

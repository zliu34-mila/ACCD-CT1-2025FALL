reflection

In this assignment, what I wanted to create were randomly floating bubbles. They exhibit a kind of nonlinear interactive movement, and their colors are different. Initially, I first drew the canvas, then influenced the bubbles' movement positions and frequency by adding noise to their X and Y axes, making the scene show a nonlinear motion. At the same time, in terms of interaction, I made their gathering center follow my mouse movement, so the scene appears to always follow my mouse.
